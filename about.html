About PedAnalyze

PedAnalyze is a web-based interface for the PedAnalyze tool, designed to streamline the annotation and analysis of pedestrian behavior in dash-cam videos. It is built to support autonomous vehicle research. It enables researchers, developers, and data scientists to create structured datasets with pre-defined tags for pedestrian and vehicle interactions, capturing critical scenarios like accidents or near-misses. With support for single-frame and multi-frame annotations, PedAnalyze reduces redundancy and enhances temporal relationship analysis, making it a valuable tool for advancing safer autonomous driving systems.

With PedAnalyze, you can:

  • Create and manage annotation projects
  • Tag pedestrian, vehicle, environment, and archetype behaviors
  • Export your annotations for further analysis

Our goal is to make behavioral annotation accessible, efficient, and reliable for everyone.
